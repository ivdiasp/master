"This app checks how secure facebook profiles of your friends are."

This app does 3 things:
- Shows whether your/your friends "public Search" option is disabled
- Shows all likes of you / of your friends and checks whether you / they were clickjacked (wikipedia.org/wiki/Clickjacking)
- Can use a google api key to google you and your friends at one go within the app.

- Get more information about Facebook Security on my blog: clickjacked.org.

Permissions
1. Is requested by every app on facebook, it cannot be "turned of" by me even though i do not need this data...
2. Permission to post on wall. Reading the wall is not possible.
3. Is used within this app to check whether you were clickjacked
4. Same as 3. but for your friends.

Check the permissions yourself: developers.facebook.com/docs/authentication/permissions/.
