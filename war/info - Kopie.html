This app will help you to check how much information is out there about you and your friends.

Go to App

This app does 3 things:
- Shows whether your/your friends "public Search" option is disabled
- shows all likes of you / of your friends
- uses a google api key to google you and your friends at one go within the app.



You can also first start the light Version, which needs only permissions 1. und 2. and is a lot faster.
