Hilf yourself and your friends:
- Facebook privacy: Check "public search" setting of you and your friends, did you / they disabled it?



- Detect clickjacking victims: Check whether you or your friends became victims of clickjacking.(http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clickjacking)



- Make use of search engines: Check what Google and "people search engines" know about you and your friends.



- Identity theft: Check whether you have "double" friends in your friend list (an attacker could set up a phony profile under the name of someone you know).



- Tell friends how to solve their issues: Post a link on their wall containing a short tutorial.

Permissions
1. Is requested by every app on Facebook, cannot be turned of
2. Permission to post on wall. Reading the wall is not possible.
3. Is used to check whether you were clickjacked (wikipedia.org/wiki/Clickjacking)
4. Same as 3. but for your friends.

Check it yourself: developers.facebook.com/docs/authentication/permissions/.
I fordere mit meiner App nur die Rechte: publish_stream,user_likes und friends_likes.
